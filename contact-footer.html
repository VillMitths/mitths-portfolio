Contact Footer Component Demo

This page demonstrates the contact footer component. The footer is fixed at the bottom of the viewport and will remain visible as you scroll.

The footer includes links to Email, GitHub, LinkedIn, and Home. It features a backdrop blur effect, responsive design, and touch-friendly targets (minimum 44px).

On mobile devices, the footer adapts to smaller screens. On very small screens (under 480px), only icons are shown to save space.

To use this component in your portfolio pages, copy the HTML footer element and the CSS from the marked section above. Don't forget to add padding-bottom: 100px; to your body element to prevent content from being hidden under the footer.
