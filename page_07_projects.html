Project Inventory

Why This Matters
For ADHD: External memory means you don't have to hold everything in your head. The system remembers so you can focus on thinking.
For chronic fatigue: Automation handles the repetitive work when energy is scarce. What took hours now takes seconds.
For anxiety: Past decisions are recorded. You don't have to second-guess what you already figured out.
For anyone drowning in information: The system filters noise, surfaces patterns, and extends your capacity to process complexity.
Local Brain
Central orchestrator. Routes requests, maintains context, coordinates agents.
Core Infrastructure
Memory Watcher
Remembers what you forget. "We tried that 2 hours ago, it failed because..." No more repeating mistakes. No more lost context. Decisions recorded, patterns extracted, anti-patterns flagged.
Memory Layer
Browser Agent
Automates the tedious. Form filling, data extraction, multi-step workflows. Visual grounding means it sees the page like you do. The hours you spent clicking become seconds.

Gmail Extractor
