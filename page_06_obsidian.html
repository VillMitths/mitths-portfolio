SECTION 05

The Second Brain

Where knowledge lives and grows
Markdown files on your machine. No cloud. No lock-in.
Bidirectional links turn notes into a knowledge graph.
Your thoughts, connected the way your mind connects them.
How It Works
Daily notes capture what happened - mood, events, energy levels, sessions completed.
Session summaries preserve decisions - what worked, what failed, patterns extracted.
Error patterns prevent repeated mistakes - solutions recorded when found.
Person notes remember context about people in your life - conversations, preferences, history.
Project notes accumulate understanding over time - architecture decisions, gotchas, design rationale.
The knowledge graph connects everything - 2000+ notes, 8000+ links, search finds not just keywords but related concepts.
Why This Matters for People
For ADHD: Your external memory never forgets where you put things. The cognitive load of "where did I see that?" disappears.
For anxiety: Decisions are recorded. No more "did I already figure this out?" or second-guessing past choices.
For chronic illness: Low-energy days still capture what matters. Voice notes become searchable knowledge.
For information overload: One trusted place for everything. No more hunting across apps, emails, bookmarks, screenshots.
